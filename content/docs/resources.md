# Resources

Tools and resources to enhance your email development workflow.

### Mailviews

[![Mailviews](/mailviews.jpg){.rounded-lg}](https://mailviews.com?ref=resources){rel=follow target=_blank}

[Mailviews](https://mailviews.com?ref=resources){rel=follow target=_blank} is the official component library for Maizzle. It's a production-ready collection of templates and reusable components that streamline your workflow and reduce build time.

### Can I Email

[caniemail.com](https://www.caniemail.com/) is _the_ tool to check email client support for HTML and CSS features.

### Email Generators

Customer.io has lots of useful [generators](https://customer.io/tools) for email development.

### Emai Client Targeting

[howtotarget.email](https://www.howtotarget.email/) is a collection of email client targeting techniques.

Maizzle has built-in support for the most important ones via Tailwind directives like `gmail:`, `ios:` or `yahoo:`. You simply use classes to add styles for specific clients.

### Testing

Render-testing your emails across popular clients is crucial to ensure they look good and work well for all your recipients. Here are some popular email testing services:

- [Testi@](https://testi.at/)
- [Litmus](https://www.litmus.com/)
- [Email on Acid](https://www.emailonacid.com/)
